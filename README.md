# 🍪 Premium Cookies Poster Design

A creative and modern poster design for a premium gourmet cookies brand. This project focuses on delivering a luxury visual experience through clean layout, rich colors, and high-quality imagery.

---

## 🎨 Project Overview

This design showcases a premium food advertisement concept, highlighting gourmet cookies with a visually appealing and elegant style.

The goal was to create a poster that:
- Captures attention instantly
- Reflects a luxury brand identity
- Enhances product presentation

---

## ✨ Features

- Clean and modern layout
- Premium color palette (navy blue & gold tones)
- Strong typography and hierarchy
- High-quality product-focused visuals
- Minimal yet impactful design

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Figma
- Typography & color theory principles

---

## 📌 Use Case

This design is ideal for:
- Bakeries & dessert brands
- Food advertisements
- Social media promotions
- Print marketing materials
